Shipping and Delivery Policy

Sync Space is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform, and all services are delivered digitally. This Shipping and Delivery Policy outlines how users gain access to our platform after subscribing.

1. Service Activation

  • Once a subscription is successfully purchased, access to Sync Space is granted immediately.
  • Users will receive a confirmation email with account details and login instructions.
  • If you do not receive an activation email, please check your spam/junk folder or contact our support team.

2. Delivery of Digital Products

  • All features and functionalities of Sync Space are accessible online via our web application.
  • There are no physical products or shipping involved.
  • Any downloadable content, such as invoices or documentation, is available within the user dashboard.

3. Account Access Issues

If you experience any difficulties accessing your account, please:

  • Ensure you are using the correct login credentials.
  • Reset your password if necessary.
  • Contact our support team for assistance.

4. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review it periodically.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@syncspace.com
